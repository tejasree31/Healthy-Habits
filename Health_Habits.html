Healthy Habits

Healthy habits are things you do daily, frequently, or routinely that
have a positive impact on your physical and mental well-being.
Habits form over time from repeating the same routines and behaviours.
Eventually, they become things you do by default or subconsciously
without having to think them through.
A healthy habit is something you do that may significantly improve an aspect of your life.

Habits of Healthy people:

  • Increase water intake
  • Keep yourself active
  • Practice gratitude
